Vision

Jake seeks to restore the balance of power between the Federal Government and states by reasserting Idaho’s powers as a sovereign state.  The founding generation of the American Republic gave the national government limited powers and gave the states broad authority.  This dynamic has been reversed and now the states are expected to obey every mandate and edict of Washington DC.  Now is the time to use state law and state resources to protect the God-given rights of our people from unconstitutional Federal overreach.
